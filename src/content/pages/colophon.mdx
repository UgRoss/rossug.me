This website is the personal site of **UgRoss**, a place for sharing thoughts, projects, and experiences.

## Technology

The site is built with [Astro](https://astro.build), a modern static site generator that delivers fast, content-focused websites. It uses the [Chiri](https://github.com/the3ash/astro-chiri) theme as a foundation, customized to fit personal preferences.

## Design

The design philosophy focuses on simplicity, readability, and performance. The site features a clean, minimal aesthetic with careful attention to typography and spacing. It supports both light and dark color schemes that adapt to your system preferences.

## Typography

The site uses system fonts for optimal performance and native feel across different platforms and devices.

## Hosting & Deployment

The site is hosted and automatically deployed through modern web infrastructure, ensuring fast load times and global availability.
